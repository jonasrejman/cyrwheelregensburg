HOW TO CONVENTION
Bring this. Have fun.

Your CYR Wheel, sleeping mat, sleeping bag, a big towel for sauna, cutlery, plate, mug and some food as your contribution to the buffet.

You can rent a wheel from us for 10 EUR per day. First come first serve. Please treat them gently.

OPEN STAGE
Got glamour?

You recently mastered new trick, a daring combo or want to practice for your next show in front of a supporting audience?

The open stage is there for you!

Go for it with all the bells and whistles, or just as you are. Only for participants of the event & friends - leave that stagefright at home and dare to be amazing! We will put you on the shedule if you get in touch. If you decide last minute - will make it work!

DETAILS
Everything else of importance.

Etiquette

There will be enough space for open training at any time - even during workshops. Take care of yourself and other CYR wheelers! Please no pillow fights past 2 am!

Meals

We all need to contribute to the buffet, please make sure you bring your favourite something so that there is enough.
On Saturday evening you can order pizzas from a restaurant nearby.
Sunday morning breakfast is on us!

Accomodation

You can sleep in the gym from Saturday to Sunday. Don't forget to bring your sleeping bag and sleeping mat! If you have a long trip and want to arrive already on Friday, please get in touch and we will work something out.

Safety

No health insurance is provided. You participate at your own risk.

Questions

Please get in touch via email at cyrwheelregensburg@web.de.
