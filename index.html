DETAILS
Everything else of importance.

Etiquette

There's always space available for open training, even during workshops. Please be mindful of yourself and other CYR wheelers. No pillow fights past 2 am!

Meals

We all need to contribute to the buffet, please make sure you bring your favourite something so that there is enough. On Saturday evening you can order pizzas from a restaurant nearby. Sunday morning breakfast is on us!

Accommodation

You can sleep in the gym from Saturday to Sunday. If you have a long trip and want to arrive already on Friday, please get in touch and we will arrange something.

Safety

No health insurance is provided. You participate at your own risk.

Questions

If you have any other questions or concerns, please email us at cyrwheelregensburg@web.de. We look forward to see you soon!
