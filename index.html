WHO
are we?

Cyr Wheel artists, gymnasts and all other kinds of people who are interested in this beautiful sport! All levels are welcome.

EACH ONE TEACH ONE
Got skills? Show and teach!

The idea of this weekend is „each one teach one“: Everybody helps everyone to learn new skills so it's up to you to give in order to receive in return. Additionally there will be the opportunity to offer a workshop - think of a topic and level so I can write it down in our schedule.

WHAT
do I need?

Your own Cyr Wheel, sleeping mat, sleeping bag, a big towel (for sauna!), cutlery, plate, mug, some food for the buffet. If you don't have a wheel, there is a limited number of wheels for rent (10€ per day). First come first serve! Please treat them gently.

OPEN STAGE
Got glamour?

We are going to offer the possibility to present whatever you feel like showing us at the Open Stage on Saturday right after the last workshop. It's not gonna be a fancy show where you need to dress up in a costume and perform your stage act (but you can!). It is more about sharing your recent success with a new trick/combo/idea/showact with a loving and supportive community or simply practicing being on stage in front of people. The Open Stage is only for participants of the convention and friends, it's gonna be a great and fun experience for us all! So don't be shy and join in :)
